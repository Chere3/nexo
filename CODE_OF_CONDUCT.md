# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating an open, welcoming, and respectful environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving and receiving feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and product

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
